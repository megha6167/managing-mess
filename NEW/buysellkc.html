About us

The year 1998 was turning point for Kanaka Catering Services business as another dynamic and an enthusiastic young person graduated from Mangalore University in commerce and was working with hotel TAJ was inspired to become a businessman joined Mr. Jagdish Shetty and both the young and energetic persons have formed a partnership firm by name Kanaka Caterers and Hospitality Services. The vision of both the youngsters was to become preferred catering services providers in the Food Management and hospitality Industry. Hard work and unlimited efforts of the partners gave returns resulted in to business growth as per the expectation of both young businessmen. Form partnership firm to private ltd. company. The year 2008 brought exploring change in their dream. Both the partners became promoters and formed a private limited company as Kanaka Food Management Services Pvt. Ltd. The incorporation of Private Limited Company has boosted the morale of the promoters and its employees. The Catering services business has spread over from one state i.e. Maharashtra to four states in the country i.e. Maharashtra, Goa, Karnataka, Orissa, today.
